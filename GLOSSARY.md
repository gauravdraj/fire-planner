# Glossary

Plain-English definitions for terms used in Fire Planner. The deployed app also shows these in the Methodology page so users do not need to open this markdown file.

- **MAGI**: Modified adjusted gross income: AGI with program-specific addbacks. Fire Planner tracks ACA MAGI and IRMAA MAGI separately where rules differ.
- **IRMAA**: Income-Related Monthly Adjustment Amount: a Medicare premium surcharge based on prior-year or prior-prior-year MAGI.
- **ACA/PTC/FPL%**: ACA marketplace premium tax credits compare household MAGI to the Federal Poverty Level, expressed as an FPL percentage.
- **LTCG**: Long-term capital gains: gains on assets held more than one year, stacked with qualified dividends into separate federal tax brackets.
- **QBI/SSTB**: Qualified business income deduction rules, including phaseouts that can limit specified service trade or business income.
- **NIIT**: Net Investment Income Tax: a 3.8% federal tax on certain investment income when MAGI exceeds statutory thresholds.
- **SEPP/72(t)**: Substantially Equal Periodic Payments under Internal Revenue Code section 72(t), used to avoid early-distribution penalties.
- **Roth conversion ladder**: A sequence of traditional-to-Roth conversions that can create later Roth principal access while managing tax brackets.
- **customLaw scenario**: A scenario-specific set of sparse law overrides layered over sealed default constants without changing the baseline constants.
